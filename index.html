    Spill The Beans

    Looking for a quick movie spoiler? Spill The Beans represents a movie spoiler app which is fun and easy to use! This app generates three random GIFs of any searched and selected movies. Users can generate a new GIF on click, and save or delete their favorite GIFs. The app features React hooks and two APIs (TMDB & Giphy) to render and update the multiple pages. This app was created with awesome friends Beatrice, Brian, and Karleen.
